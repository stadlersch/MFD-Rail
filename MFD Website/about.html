About us

Our Mission

As a new and genuine alternative provider of intermodal wagons "fresh blood", MFD Rail focuses exclusively on the procurement, rental and maintenance of intermodal wagons and advises its customers and other business partners as a competent, European acting "intermodal specialist dealer".

We are building our intermodal business on the green field using the "greenfield approach", which enables us to orient all resources such as personnel, processes and systems exclusively to the intermodal business in order to offer the customer "intermodal-tailored" services.

The state-of-the-art systems newly procured and introduced for this purpose secure MFD Rail's sustainable technological lead and establish MFD Rail as the most technologically available lessor in Europe.

The desired “data leadership” enables MFD Rail to provide modern, digital communication with customers, suppliers and other stakeholders in a way that will shape the industry.

We build our business model, our business partners and our business activities in such a way that MFD Rail as «ESG compliant», i.e. is perceived as an environmentally friendly, social lessor with a prudent, sustainable management and preferred lessor in the market.

Our customer service meets the highest requirements «lessor with hightest customer service» and represents recognizable added value through transparency, fairness, technology and efficiency.

The stringent focus on our core business, Intermodal, allows us to gain market leadership in terms of scalability and efficiency «market leader in efficiency». The scalability achieved leads to low transaction costs, "cost leadership", which MFD Rail is granted a sustainable competitive advantage and results in competitive rental prices.
We will become no 2 lessor of intermodal railcars within continental Europe.
